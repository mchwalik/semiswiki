## Overview
Coherent is one of the most direct public-market expressions of the indium-phosphide laser bottleneck inside AI optics, spanning laser capacity, transceiver demand, and the broader CPO/silicon-photonics transition.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Sits beside [[lumentum]] as a core company-level expression of the InP laser and optics squeeze described in [[photonics-cpo-supply-chain]].
- Benefits if AI networking demand keeps pulling scarce laser and epitaxy capacity toward higher-performance optical links.
- Helps connect the visible transceiver winners to the less visible upstream material and equipment constraints discussed in [[rating-ai-supply-chain-bottlenecks]].

## Key angles
- The interview explicitly places Coherent among the laser-capacity names buying more InP manufacturing equipment.
- Its importance rises as CPO and 1.6T optics increase demands on linewidth, noise performance, and laser power.
- Risk is that investor enthusiasm overstates near-term conversion of optical demand into incremental profitable capacity.
