## Overview
Synopsys is described as one half of the core EDA duopoly and as a strategic software toll collector on rising chip complexity, custom silicon proliferation, and increasingly simulation-heavy design workflows.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Provides the software/control-plane complement to physical bottlenecks discussed in [[advanced-packaging]].
- Benefits from the same structural rise in AI hardware complexity that drives demand for [[nvidia]] and upstream semiconductor suppliers.
- Helps connect the wiki’s hardware theses to the design, verification, and digital-twin layer.
