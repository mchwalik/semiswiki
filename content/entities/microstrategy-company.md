## Overview
MicroStrategy is treated as a financial-engineering wrapper around bitcoin exposure whose fast-changing capital structure can create mispricings relative to the underlying asset and its own preferred or structured instruments.

## Relevance in this wiki

- Risk is that capital-structure complexity changes quickly and invalidates static valuation work.
