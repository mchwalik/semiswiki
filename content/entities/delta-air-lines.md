## Overview
Delta is included as a preferred airline beneficiary of World Cup travel demand, where already-strong international flows could get an extra pricing and load-factor boost during the peak summer travel window.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Acts as one of the favored airline expressions inside [[world-cup]].
- Pairs naturally with [[united-airlines]] as a mainline-carrier beneficiary rather than a speculative one-off transport idea.
- Adds travel-traffic sensitivity to the broader event-driven watchlist in [[26-trades-for-2026]].

## Key angles
- The source treats the World Cup as helpful but not thesis-changing for Delta.
- Incremental event demand matters most because it lands during a season when pricing power is already high.
- Risk is that the catalyst remains too small relative to Delta’s overall network and earnings base.
