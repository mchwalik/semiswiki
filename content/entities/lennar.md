## Overview
Lennar appears as another large US homebuilder positioned to benefit from lower domestic rates and a healthier housing turnover backdrop.

## Relevance in this wiki

- Helps turn the annual watchlist into a navigable entity layer rather than a set of abstract baskets.

## Key angles
- A large-cap way to express the US-housing side of the macro divergence trade.
- The company benefits if refinancing and affordability improve.
- Risk is that macro easing fails to translate into real demand acceleration.
