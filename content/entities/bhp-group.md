## Overview
BHP Group appears as one of the listed mining beneficiaries of a world still short of long-dated commodity supply after years of underinvestment, permitting friction, and cost inflation.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Named in [[post-traumatic-supply-disorder]] as a way to express scarce resource supply.
- Adds a diversified mining entity to a framework previously captured mostly as a concept.
- Helps connect industrial scarcity to listed equity expressions.

## Key angles
- The thesis is less about spot commodity excitement and more about structural supply difficulty.
- Large incumbents can benefit when new supply is expensive, slow, and politically hard.
- Risk is that macro demand weakens before supply scarcity gets fully monetized.
