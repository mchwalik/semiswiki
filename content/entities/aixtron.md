## Overview
AIXTRON is positioned as an upstream equipment supplier with relevance to compound semiconductors and photonics manufacturing, especially where MOCVD capacity matters.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Fits the core idea in [[photonics-cpo-supply-chain]] that the best asymmetry may sit in tooling rather than in the most obvious optical endpoints.
- Bridges optical and wide-bandgap manufacturing themes across [[ai-materials]] and [[ai-power-supply-chain-inheritance]].
- Benefits if AI demand pulls forward more specialized process steps that depend on scarce equipment know-how.
