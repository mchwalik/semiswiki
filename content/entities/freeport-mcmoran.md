## Overview
Freeport-McMoRan is cited as a copper-linked beneficiary of a regime where major new supply is difficult and expensive to bring online, making existing scale assets more valuable over time.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Explicitly named in [[post-traumatic-supply-disorder]].
- Adds a more focused copper expression versus diversified miners.
- Connects old-economy scarcity to electrification and AI-adjacent infrastructure needs.

## Key angles
- Copper supply constraints raise the value of existing, scalable assets.
- The thesis depends on long-cycle supply barriers, not just short-term price moves.
- Risk is that cyclical demand weakness overwhelms the structural argument temporarily.
