## Overview
Boeing is framed as the beaten-down long leg of a contrarian aerospace pair trade, with the thesis centered on operational normalization, financial recovery, and upside from expectations that are already washed out.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Represents the long side of [[long-boeing-short-airbus]].
- Shows up in the wiki as a recovery-versus-expectations setup rather than a generic aerospace holding.
- Anchors one of the cleaner relative-value ideas in [[26-trades-for-2026]].

## Key angles
- Upside comes from partial recovery rather than heroic execution assumptions.
- The trade works best if operating and delivery performance improve while the market still discounts Boeing heavily.
- Risk is that execution problems persist longer than the valuation can tolerate.
