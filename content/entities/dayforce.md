## Overview
Dayforce appears as a payroll and HCM platform with on-demand-pay functionality, making it a software-distribution expression of the earned-wage-access thesis.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Acts as a concrete company expression of [[earned-wage-access]].
- Helps turn the annual watchlist into a navigable entity layer rather than a set of abstract baskets.

- It widens the thesis from consumer apps to enterprise HR software.
- Risk is that EWA remains only one feature inside a broader HCM valuation.
