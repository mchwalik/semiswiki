Sims is framed as a recycler and IT asset-recovery business that can monetize the eventual turnover of AI servers, accelerators, storage, and memory-heavy datacenter hardware.

- Links back to [[inference-on-device]] and broader compute proliferation because more hardware deployed today becomes future recovery volume.
