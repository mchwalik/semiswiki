## Overview
Airbus is used as the short leg against Boeing because it enters the pair trade with better perceived quality, richer expectations, and a higher risk of narrative slippage if execution or supply-chain pressure worsens.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Represents the short side of [[long-boeing-short-airbus]].
- Matters less as a standalone bearish call than as the expensive comparator versus Boeing.
- Helps the wiki capture relative-value setups, not only outright longs.

## Key angles
- The thesis depends on expectations being too comfortable relative to future delivery friction.
- Airbus is the benchmark that makes Boeing’s recovery asymmetry more visible.
- Risk is that Airbus continues executing well enough that the short leg offsets Boeing’s improvement.
