## Overview
Wolfspeed is used as the flagship wide-bandgap case study: a battered silicon-carbide specialist whose EV-driven overbuild may become strategically useful again as AI data centers move toward higher-voltage power architectures.

## Relevance in this wiki
- Serves as the clearest single-stock illustration of [[ai-power-supply-chain-inheritance]].
- Extends the [[ai-materials]] argument by showing how upstream material and wafer capability can be mispriced when legacy end-markets are weak.
- Sits one layer below [[nvidia]], supplying the kind of power-stack transition future dense racks may require.
