## Summary
This moonshot idea examines whether high-value materials or components can eventually be manufactured more efficiently in orbit than on Earth. The uploaded PDF makes the implementation layer much more explicit by showing a Space Supply Chain Basket spanning orbital logistics, space traffic control, new aerospace manufacturers, and space-defense infrastructure.
