## Summary
This parent bucket groups trades that revolve around scarce physical capacity the market may have underpriced: shipyards, launch/logistics systems, commodity and transport supply, fleet wear, and other hard-to-rebuild industrial bottlenecks. It is the best umbrella for the wiki’s strategic old-economy and moonshot-capacity concepts.

## Why this second-layer bucket exists
- The corpus increasingly mixes AI with harder industrial and geopolitical capacity trades.
- Several concepts are really variants of the same meta-framework: scarcity in physical systems that take years to rebuild and suddenly become strategically valuable.

## Boundary rules
- Use this bucket when the thesis depends on constrained physical capacity, long rebuild times, or industrial bottlenecks.
- Use [[policy-rates-and-geopolitics]] when the bottleneck matters less than the policy regime itself.
- Use [[ai-and-agentic-stack]] when the bottleneck is still inside the AI compute and connectivity stack.
