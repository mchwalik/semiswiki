## Summary
This section argues that household cash-flow stress creates fertile ground for earned-wage-access products and related financial tools. The uploaded PDF shows that the live implementation is broader than a narrow fintech app thesis: it spans payroll software, consumer-liquidity rails, pawn, installment credit, and adjacent payments infrastructure.

## Notable expressions
- Direct or high-beta expressions include [[dave-inc]], [[enova-international]], [[world-acceptance]], [[firstcash-holdings]], and [[ezcorp]].
- Payroll and workflow distributors include [[dayforce]], [[automatic-data-processing]], [[paylocity]], and [[paycom]].
- Adjacent consumer-liquidity rails include [[green-dot]], [[western-union]], [[block]], [[upbound-group]], and [[gendigital]].
