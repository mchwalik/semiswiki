## Summary
This trade looks for small-cap financials with disproportionate upside to rate cuts, funding relief, or a more favorable yield backdrop. The memo frames certain regionals and adjacent financial firms as mispriced beneficiaries of easing conditions.

## Key angles
- Contributing Author: Inspector Cluseau
- Long Rate Sensitive Regional Banks (OPBK, WSBF, KRNY)
- Long Oppenheimer

## Citrindex basket constituents
- The live sleeve is more focused than the memo's broader expression list: [[oppenheimer-holdings]], Waterstone Financial, Kearny Financial, and OP Bancorp.
