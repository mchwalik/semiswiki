## Summary
This third-layer bucket groups themes where value accrues through durable technical platforms, reusable stacks, and compounding infrastructure rather than short-lived macro triggers. These are the wiki’s most architecture-driven and system-design-heavy trades.

## Second-layer parents in this bucket
- [[ai-and-agentic-stack]]

## What belongs here
- AI compute, bandwidth, power, packaging, and agentic utility frameworks.
- Biotech ideas where the edge comes from repeatable platform logic rather than one binary catalyst.

## What does not belong here
- Election, rates, sanctions, or policy-path trades belong under [[regime-policy-and-scarcity]].
- Household cash-flow or taste-driven spending trades belong under [[consumer-behavior-and-demand-routing]].
