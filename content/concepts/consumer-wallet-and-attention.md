## Summary
This parent bucket groups trades that depend on how constrained consumers allocate cash, attention, aspiration, and convenience. It sits one level above the individual concepts that focus on liquidity stress, small discretionary splurges, cultural pull, or low-friction food spend.

## Why this second-layer bucket exists
- The wiki had multiple consumer-facing ideas, but they were split between tactical spending pulses, household liquidity stress, and culture-led demand without a common umbrella.
- This page clarifies that these are not generic consumer-discretionary ideas; they are mostly about bifurcated demand and behavioral allocation under pressure.

## Child concepts in this bucket
- Cash-flow and liquidity stress: [[earned-wage-access]]
- Tactical spending pulses: [[bread-and-circuses]]
- Convenience and operating-efficiency food trade: [[slop-bowl-economics]]
- Taste, status, and female-consumer cultural signal: [[the-girlfriend-index]]

## Boundary rules
- Use this bucket when the core driver is household cash timing, spending trade-offs, convenience, or culturally mediated purchase intent.
- Use [[policy-rates-and-geopolitics]] if the main mechanism is elections, interest rates, or macro policy.
- Use [[ai-and-agentic-stack]] if the thesis is mainly about compute, infrastructure, or software automation rather than end-consumer allocation.
