## Summary
This section examines how fast-casual and value-focused food chains respond to pressured consumers and rising labor intensity. The uploaded PDF turns the idea into a clearer two-layer basket: merchants such as [[chipotle-mexican-grill]], [[cava-group]], and [[sweetgreen]], plus restaurant-automation enablers like [[middleby]], [[fanuc]], [[compass-group]], and [[rockwell-automation]].

## Key angles
- The merchant side is about repeatable, throughput-friendly food concepts that can still capture constrained consumers.
- The hardware and automation side is about reducing labor intensity and improving consistency.
- The theme sits near [[next-gen-autonomous]] because service automation and robotics are part of the operating model, not just a demand story.
