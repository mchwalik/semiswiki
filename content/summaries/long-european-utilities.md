## Summary
This trade ties European utilities to gas, LNG, and regional power-market dynamics rather than to a simple defensive-equity narrative. The setup depends on changing energy spreads and policy conditions creating a better earnings backdrop than investors expect, with [[iberdrola]], [[enel]], and [[e-on]] called out as primary beneficiaries.
