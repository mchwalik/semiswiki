## Summary
This source is a broad annual watchlist spanning AI, policy, financials, global macro, robotics, biotech, consumer, pair trades, and moonshots. The uploaded original PDF adds important graphic-only implementation detail: several pages contain basket screenshots and tables that substantially expand the wiki’s entity layer for [[shipbuilding]], [[earned-wage-access]], [[slop-bowl-economics]], [[orbital-manufacturing]], [[dovish-fed-hawkish-ecb]], and [[ai-materials]].

## What it adds to the wiki
- Shows that Citrini's 2026 slate extends earlier AI infrastructure work into endpoint inference, packaging, upstream materials, and operational bottlenecks.
- Places previously ingested theses like [[photonics-cpo-supply-chain]] and [[ai-power-supply-chain-inheritance]] inside a larger annual basket process.
- Adds a deeper basket-and-entity implementation layer for macro, policy, consumer-liquidity, maritime, and moonshot sections that looked thinner in the markdown-only pass.
