## Summary
This source is not just a meta-introduction once the PDF extract is included; it is a broad annual watchlist spanning AI, policy, financials, global macro, robotics, biotech, consumer, and pair trades. For this wiki's domain, the most relevant sections are the AI-adjacent frameworks around [[inference-on-device]], [[advanced-packaging]], and [[ai-materials]].

## What it adds to the wiki
- Shows that Citrini's 2026 slate extends earlier AI infrastructure work into endpoint inference, packaging, and upstream material bottlenecks.
- Places previously ingested theses like [[photonics-cpo-supply-chain]] and [[ai-power-supply-chain-inheritance]] inside a larger annual basket process.
- Adds broader context that many AI-related ideas are being expressed as thematic baskets or selective single-name expressions rather than narrow one-stock calls.

## Notable relevant sections
- **Inference on Device:** argues that always-on agent use, latency, and hardware progress support more edge and local inference.
- **Advanced Packaging:** treats packaging as a strategic AI bottleneck and highlights architecture-driven constraints.
- **AI Materials:** pushes the AI trade further upstream into specialized material and process suppliers.
