## Summary
This section explores a rare policy divergence scenario in which the Fed eases faster than the ECB. The uploaded PDF turns that macro view into a much more concrete equity map: long US refi and housing-activity beneficiaries like [[d-r-horton]], [[lennar]], [[pultegroup]], [[toll-brothers]], [[pennymac-financial]], [[rocket-companies]], [[fidelity-national-financial]], [[first-american-financial]], [[builders-firstsource]], and [[zillow-group]], while remaining cautious on European leveraged property and indebted cyclicals such as [[vonovia]], [[unibail-rodamco-westfield]], [[cellnex]], [[telecom-italia]], [[fraport]], [[tui]], and [[alstom]].
